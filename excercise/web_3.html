Four frames

A butterfly is a captivating insect known for its vibrant, colorful wings and graceful flight. Belonging to the order Lepidoptera, butterflies undergo a remarkable transformation through four stages of their life cycle: egg, larva (caterpillar), pupa (chrysalis), and adult. They are found in a wide range of habitats, including forests, grasslands, and gardens, thriving on nectar from flowers. Beyond their beauty, butterflies play a vital role in pollination, contributing to the health of ecosystems. Their delicate wings, often adorned with intricate patterns, make them a symbol of transformation, freedom, and resilience in various cultures around the world.
